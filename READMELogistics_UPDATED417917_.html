Course Overview

Logistics is cool when you have specific problems to focus on and you only have problems to focus on when you are directly involved with logistics. So this course, instead of delivering the same information you can look up by yourself, will be focused on presenting you with common logistics problems and have you find solutions and build tools for those solutions.

Proficiency is not required! Competency and understanding are!

We are always focused on the 4 C’s:

  • Critical Thinking
  • Communication
  • Collaboration
  • Creativity
  • Honesty
